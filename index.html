Migration and restructuring services

We serve small-sizes companies in all industries with high quality migration consulting services

Migration design

Our team of enthusiastic architects will analyse and evaluate your environment and business goals to envision and design the optimal migration strategy.

Migration setup and coexistence

Once the migration design process is completed our staff will help you on the deployment of the migration toolkit proposed, enabling the coexistence between the different environments and test, validate and automate the migration procedures.

Migration execution

For the repetitive and time-consuming bulk migration, we usually work hand-by-hand with System Integrators in your country to guarantee the delivery.
